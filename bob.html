About Bob the Baker Baking Company Donut Maker is inspired by Cookie Clicker About the Developer Home


Bob the Baker was tired of making real donuts and was ready to retire. He needed help to keep his love of donuts alive. He wanted someone to create a game that uses button clicks to create virtual donuts for the world to enjoy.

But all those early mornings of donut making may have tired out his brain because Bob had some strange requirements for things like 'Auto Clickers' and 'Donut Multipliers'.

Unfortunately, it's always 'Time to make the Donuts' and Bob is too busy to give us guidance on how to implement these requirements. So we've abliged him here are much as possible. We hope you enjoy playing the Donut Maker as much as we did in creating it.
